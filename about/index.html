About

Named after scientist Johannes Diderik van der Waals, Wan der Vaal seeks to be a desktop launcher for Information Systems, Work Resources, and their related documentation.

Electron was chosen because of its ability to forward links of not only http:// protocol, but also file:// and custom as well. Urls can be passed to the operating system shell, allowing local programs on the computer or Office documents on a NAS or Shared Resource to be opened without Internet Explorer with lowered security settings.

Angular-Material was chosen because of angular’s filter and sort services and Material design’s “Card” layout a good responsive layout. Because of Material Design’s proliferation throughout the internet, many users will be comfortable with this user interface from Android applications, Google Applications, and many other Web Applications. Material Design also brings over UI idioms and interactive controls that allow a lot of information to be packed tightly into a small amount of space.
